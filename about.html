Our Mission

Our Community Service Project titled "Unveiling the Chemicals on Our Fruits and Vegetables" focuses on raising awareness about the harmful effects of chemical usage in agriculture and promoting the importance of organic farming.

In recent years, the use of chemical pesticides and preservatives has increased rapidly in fruit and vegetable production. While they help in keeping produce looking fresh and increasing yields, they also pose serious health risks to consumers and long-term damage to the soil and environment.
