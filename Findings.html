Vendor Perspective

Vendors are aware of the health effects of chemicals, but they are not willing to sell organic produce as they don't get enough profit. There is also no demand for organic items in the market.

üí° Key Conclusions

The main reason for chemical usage in farming is the lack of awareness about health effects and the lack of demand for organic items in the market. Based on these findings, we decided to create awareness about the effects of these chemicals on health and provide alternatives to farmers and vendors to switch to organic farming and selling.
